Oden

Description

Oden is a type of nabemono, consisting of several ingredients such as boiled eggs, daikon, konjac, and processed fishcakes stewed in a light, soy-flavored dashi broth.

Ingredients

  • 8-inch daikon radish
  • 5 large eggs
  • 5 strips dried seaweed
  • 4 oz octopus sashimi
  • 1 package konnyaku
  • 1 package tokyo negi
  • 2 packages japanese fish cakes
  • 1 aburaage
  • 1 kirimochi or homemade mochi
  • 1 inch carrot

Steps

  1. put everything together
  2. boil 'em
  3. mash 'em
  4. sitck 'em in a stew
